Our Projects

Children are the future of our world. Their progress is the progress of humanity. It is a challenging time for many a child to pursue their dreams, ambitions, goals and get a good education due to poverty, socio-economic factors, war, famine - all of which were further accentuated by the pandemic.  ​ In India, the impact of the pandemic on the learning loss of children in vulnerable/ marginalized segments of the society has been massive during the pandemic and it continues according to various reports.  ​ At Square World, we are taking small steps to alleviate the problems of such children by creating an ecosystem of opportunities for them, empowering their quest for education and advancing their academic aspirations to unlock their true potential. ​
