A bit of background

Everyone deserves a fair and equitable opportunity to pursue their aspirations and achieve their full potential. everyone can pursue their aspirations due to their current socioeconomic status. Unequal access to information and resources limits their ability to make informed choices to help them achieve their potential. The divide in society to pursue opportunities has been further widened by the socioeconomic effects of the pandemic. The pandemic has spotlighted the critical role played by technology and access to the internet for the common man to lead a fulfilling life. This is a definitive inflection point that brought to light to the general public and world at large the great digital divide and digital poverty in socioeconomically poor groups for education.

Our Focus

Equitable

At Square World Foundation, we are dedicated to ensuring that every child has an equal opportunity to pursue their aspirations.

Access

 We will work to ensure that access to information, technology, internet and resources are available to the socioeconomically marginalized.

Quality

We strive to ask the difficult question of why not every child can get the best quality of education irrespective of their location or demographics.

Democracy

Ensure that education is driven through a democratic approach, where authentic information is available to every child with choices and they are making independent & informed decisions on academic/ career.
